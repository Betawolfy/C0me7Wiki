Economy

In order to rewards guild members, we decided to create an economy.

⚠️ All pictures are from the beta version of the bot. Changes can be made with time.

How to start your economy journey?

Everything is setup so when staff will give you money, you'll directly have an account.
From here, you can navigate through the commands.

I. Money

This is the principal thing of all the economy system.
Our currency is called Tix or Tixes in plurial. the icon of the currency is: .
There is many way of winning Tixes:

  • By attending activities.
  • By making commissions.
  • By using /explore or /adventure. (read the wiki here)

here is all money commands:

1. Balance

Syntax: /balance [user] • No permission required.

Allows you to see your balance or another member one.

2. Give money request

Syntax: /give-request [member] [amount] [reason] • No permission required.

Since it will be not allowed to re-sell colorpacks and addons, so we'll watch give money request, like a real bank!
The command will allows your to make a request to give money to another player.
The request will be sent to the staff, and they will decide if they accept or not.
If the request is accepted, you'll receive the Tix, however if it's refused the person who made the request will keep the money.

3. Leaderboard

Syntax: /eco-leaderboard • No permission required.

Display the amount of Tix for all members from the highest amount to the lowest amount.

4. economy stats

Syntax: /economy • No permission required.

Display how much Tix are in circulation on the guild.
