How to start an adventure?

Enlisted will have access to a command: /explore for Explorers; /adventure for Adventurers.

How Will it work?

When each one of the command are executed: an random choosen scenario will launch (these scenarios are created by staff.)

Exemple of a scenario:

"While exploring a village, You find a cave. Maybe it contains some useful items. What do you choose?"

And then, 2 or 3 reactions will popup, letting the player to make a choice:
